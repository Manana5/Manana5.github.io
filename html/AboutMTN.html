Launched in 1994, the MTN Group Limited (MTN Group) is a multinational telecommunications group, with its core operations in 21 countries in Africa and the Middle East. As at the end of December 2008 MTN recorded more than 90 700 000 subscribers.


MTN Swaziland LTD is a subsidiary of the MTN Group and started operating in September 1998. Since its inception, MTN Swaziland has developed to become one of Swaziland's most successful and prominent businesses.


By October 2013, it had 810 000 subscribers. The geographical network coverage was last measured at 96% and population coverage is at 98%. The network is currently available on 2G, 3G & 4G.


The cellular network covers over 70% of the geographical area, including all major business centres, as well as continuous coverage from the Ngwenya border, along the Mbabane-Manzini highway and on to the Mozambique border.

21 Days of Yello Care is MTN Group's largest CSI project where each operating country (OpCo) is required to use 21 days of a month to engage in community service. They are incentivised by awards and cash rewards for excellence, amongst other criteria.

2017 proved a very awarding year for MTN Swaziland. By coming first place, through out all 27 OpCos, the company won a grand price of $100 000 and a beautiful trophy. In other years, Swazi MTN has received 2nd place in the South-Eastern region.
